A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering.

Before his presidency, he worked as an aerospace engineer with Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).

He was awarded the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honour, in 1997.
